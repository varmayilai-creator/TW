Travel Information

Public Transport: The centre is accessible via bus routes from Narre Warren and Berwick train stations. Please check PTV for the most up-to-date routes and timetables.

Parking: Ample free parking is available on-site at the Strathaird Family & Community Centre car park.

Onam Celebration Programs

  • 10:00 AM - Welcome & Pookkalam Laying: Join us as we collaboratively create a vibrant Onam Pookkalam (floral carpet), symbolising welcome and prosperity.
  • 11:00 AM - Mahabali's Grand Entry: Witness the traditional arrival of King Mahabali, a highlight of Onam festivities.
  • 11:30 AM - Traditional Onam Games: Participate in lively games like Tug-of-War (Vadamvali) and other fun activities for all ages.
  • 1:00 PM - Onasadya (Grand Feast): Indulge in the sumptuous traditional Onam Sadya, a vegetarian feast served on banana leaves. (Please refer to the 'Sadya Items' tab for details on contributions).
  • 2:30 PM - Cultural Performances: Enjoy captivating performances showcasing Kerala's rich art forms, including traditional dances and music.
  • 4:00 PM - Farewell & Networking: A chance to mingle, share stories, and bid farewell until next time.

(Program schedule is subject to minor changes.)
